Task 2

The British Psychological Society (BPS) is our accrediting body and promotes excellence in Psychology through practice and study. The BPS publishes articles throughout the year and in 2018 the society asked its members to answer the question “Why do we need psychology? And what does psychology need?” and presented the winning responses in one of it’s articles.

  1. Before you come to university, we would like you to read this article (https://www.bps.org.uk/psychologist/why-do-we-need-psychology-and-what- does-psychology-need)
  2. Select one contributor whose input interests you

We hope you find these short tasks interesting and useful to orientate you to your Psychology degree. We will be in touch again before the start of induction week.
